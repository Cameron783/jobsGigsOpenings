About JIG:


Active since 2024

A Gig, Internship and Job Finder exists to be a quick access platform informing users about job, internship and gig openings, while also providing valuable tips for job interviews and career advancement. Our mission is to empower individuals by connecting them with opportunities that align with their skills, interests, and career aspirations.

Even if having a Job, you can be kept updated on gig openings and possible part-time jobs if the situation arises. Teenagers can also use this site in case of searching for a summer job.
